Local Warriors

It's a common platform for all the people working individualy or in groups or in NGOs in or around BHU helping others in whichever way possible. Its a guiding portal for the students or staff of BHU who want to indulge in any social cause but don't know where to go.So here you can find NGOs based on your interest fields.

Social Events

Here we provide you information about all the upcoming events of social causes in or around BHU. Either you are organising any camp and need volunteers or you wanna spend your sunday helping underprivileged,join us and make a call for your event and get to know about many others.
